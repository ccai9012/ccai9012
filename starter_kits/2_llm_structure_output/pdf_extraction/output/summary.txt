Based on the provided text, here is a summary of the described energy plan.

**Location:**
The project is located in **Aniak, Alaska**.

**Main Objectives:**
The overall goal is to create an **Energy Action Plan** for the Aniak Traditional Council. The key objectives are:
*   Improve energy efficiency in tribal buildings.
*   Summarize energy audit recommendations.
*   Provide actionable steps to guide future retrofit projects.
*   Help the Tribe achieve its overall energy goals.

**Actions:**
The project followed a specific procedure, which included these key actions:
1.  **Holding a Kick-off Meeting:** Discussing project goals with the Tribal Council and developing a data monitoring plan to track energy use and occupant comfort.
2.  **Conducting Energy Audits:** Energy Audits of Alaska performed assessments of the tribal buildings to identify energy efficiency measures.
3.  **Developing the Energy Action Plan:** Creating this document, which includes audit summaries, a maintenance plan, and information on funding and training opportunities.
4.  **Community Outreach:** Presenting the project at conferences and creating flyers and a video for the community.
5.  **Coordination:** Communicating with local energy programs to coordinate with other regional projects.

**Stakeholders:**
The main stakeholders involved are:
*   **Aniak Traditional Council:** The primary partner and project lead.
*   **Cold Climate Housing Research Center (CCHRC):** A key project organizer and author of the plan.
*   **Energy Audits of Alaska:** The company that conducted the building energy audits.
*   **Nuvista Electric Light & Power / CEMAI (Bertha Prince):** A local energy program coordinator for regional collaboration.
*   **United States Department of Energy Office of Indian Energy:** The federal agency providing support and funding for the project.
