Here’s a summary of the **Aniak Energy Action Plan 2019** based on the provided context:

### **Location**  
- **Aniak, Alaska** – The project focuses on tribal buildings in the Aniak community.

### **Main Objectives**  
1. **Improve energy efficiency** in tribal buildings through audits, retrofits, and maintenance plans.  
2. **Reduce energy consumption** by identifying cost-effective measures.  
3. **Develop a long-term Energy Action Plan** to guide future efficiency projects.  
4. **Enhance data monitoring** to track energy use and occupant comfort.  
5. **Secure funding and training** for retrofits and maintenance.  

### **Key Actions**  
1. **Energy Audits** – Conducted by Energy Audits of Alaska to assess tribal buildings and recommend efficiency measures.  
2. **Data Monitoring Plan** – Track energy use and comfort metrics in participating buildings.  
3. **Maintenance Plan** – Provide checklists for staff to implement low-cost energy-saving measures.  
4. **Funding & Training** – Identify financing opportunities and local contractors for retrofits.  
5. **Outreach & Reporting** – Share project progress with the community and submit reports to the **U.S. Department of Energy (DOE)**.  

### **Stakeholders**  
1. **Aniak Traditional Council** – Provided building-specific goals and broader energy objectives.  
2. **Cold Climate Housing Research Center (CCHRC)** – Assisted with project coordination and planning.  
3. **Energy Audits of Alaska** – Conducted building assessments and efficiency recommendations.  
4. **Nuvista Electric Light & Power / CEMAI** – Collaborated on regional energy projects (via Bertha Prince, Energy Program Coordinator).  
5. **U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Office of Indian Energy** – Provided funding and received quarterly reports.  
6. **Local Contractors & Maintenance Staff** – Identified as key players in implementing retrofits and maintenance tasks.  

### **Additional Notes**  
- The plan includes **appendix materials** such as audit summaries, outreach flyers, and contractor scopes of work.  
- The project was presented at **DOE Office of Indian Energy Program Reviews (2017 & 2018)**.
